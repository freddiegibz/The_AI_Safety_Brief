Why staying safe feels harder than it used to

Most people are doing the same things they've always done online. Checking emails, answering calls, shopping, banking, keeping in touch.

What's changed isn't you

What's changed is how convincing some messages now look.

Some emails feel genuine until they aren't. Some calls sound urgent and familiar. Some websites look right at first glance.

The usual warning signs are quieter

That doesn't mean people are becoming careless. It means the usual warning signs are quieter than they used to be.

As a result, sensible people find themselves hesitating more often — wondering what to trust and what to ignore.
